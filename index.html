About

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate at City University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Cong Wang and Prof. Hong Xu, . I have been a visiting researcher at The University of Texas at Austin, under the supervision of Prof. Aditya Akella for nine months in 2023. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong in 2019. My research interests lie primarily in the area of Systems for Machine Learning (SysML).

[Open to job opportunities] I am in the job market with the expected graduation in Spring 2024. I am currently searching for job openings in the industry and open to relocation.

  • Distributed training & inference: Accelerating large-scale distributed DNN tasks
