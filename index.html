Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at City University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Hong Xu, Henry and Prof. Cong Wang. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong in 2019. My research interests lie primarily in the area of distributed machine learning system (MLSys).

Research Interest

  • Resource scheduling, schedule jobs/tasks/executors with maximum efficiency

  • Distributed training, faster and easier distributed training for large-scale ML

  • Simulation, accurate and efficient performance simulation of distributed training workloads
